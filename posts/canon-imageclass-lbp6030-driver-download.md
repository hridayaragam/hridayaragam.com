- **Print Jobs Stuck in Queue**: Sometimes, print jobs may get stuck in the queue. This can often be resolved by restarting the printer and the computer. If the issue persists, cancel all print jobs and try printing again.

- **Error Messages**: Be attentive to any error messages the printer displays. Often, these messages will provide insight into specific issues, such as low toner or paper jams, which may need manual resolution.
