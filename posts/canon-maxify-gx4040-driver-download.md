## Common Issues and Solutions

Despite its robust design, users may encounter common issues. Here are some typical problems and their solutions:

### Issue 1: Printer Not Recognized
**Solution**: Ensure that the driver is properly installed and that the printer is correctly connected to the computer. Restart both the printer and computer to refresh the connection.

### Issue 2: Poor Print Quality
**Solution**: Check the ink levels and clean the print head through the printer maintenance settings. Make sure you are using the correct paper type for the desired print quality.

### Issue 3: Paper Jams
**Solution**: Open the printer cover and carefully remove the jammed paper. Check the paper tray for proper loading and make sure not to overload it.

### Issue 4: Connectivity Issues
**Solution**: If using a wireless connection, ensure that the printer is connected to the correct Wi-Fi network. Reset the network settings in the printer if necessary.
