## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the right driver, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

1. **Printer Not Recognized**: Ensure that the printer is properly connected to your computer or network. If using a USB connection, try a different port or cable. Restart both the printer and the computer.

2. **Slow Printing Speed**: Check your printer settings to make sure you're not using a high-quality print mode unless necessary. Sometimes, changing to a draft mode can significantly improve speed for less critical prints.

3. **Quality Issues**: If prints are coming out blurry or streaked, ensure that the printhead is clean. You can also check your ink levels and perform a nozzle check if necessary.

4. **Inability to Print Wirelessly**: Confirm that the printer is connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your computer. Check network settings and re-enter any Wi-Fi passwords if necessary.
