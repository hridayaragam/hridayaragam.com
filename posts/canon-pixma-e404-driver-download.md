## Common Issues and Solutions

Like any piece of technology, users may occasionally encounter issues with the Canon PIXMA E404 printer. Here are some common problems and their potential solutions:

### 1. Printer Not Recognized
- **Solution**: Ensure that the USB cable is securely connected or that the printer is connected to the correct Wi-Fi network. Restart both the printer and computer to resolve connectivity issues.

### 2. Poor Print Quality
- **Solution**: Check the ink levels and replace or refill cartridges as necessary. Additionally, cleaning the print head through the printer software can enhance output quality.

### 3. Paper Jams
- **Solution**: Gently remove any jammed paper from the printer. Regularly check and ensure that the paper is loaded correctly and is of the right type and size specified by the printer.

### 4. Driver Issues
- **Solution**: Sometimes, driver updates can resolve performance issues. Uninstall the existing driver and download the latest version from a trusted source.

### 5. Slow Printing
- **Solution**: Check for print queue and clear any pending documents. Adjust print settings to prioritize speed over quality for quicker outputs.
