## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the correct driver installed, users may encounter some common issues while using the Canon PIXMA E460. Here are some typical problems and their solutions:

- **Printer Not Recognized:** If your computer does not recognize the printer, ensure the driver is correctly installed. Restart both the printer and your computer to reset any communication errors. Confirm that the USB or network connection is secure.

- **Connection Issues:** For Wi-Fi connectivity problems, recheck your network settings and ensure that the printer is connected to the correct Wi-Fi network. Reinstalling the driver may also help address connectivity issues.
