### Issue: Printer Not Recognized

**Solution**: Ensure that the printer is properly connected to the computer. If using a USB connection, check the cable and port for damages. For wireless connections, confirm that the printer and computer are on the same network.

**Solution**: Open the *Devices and Printers* section in your computer settings and cancel any stuck print jobs. Restart the printer and try printing again.

**Solution**: Check the ink levels and perform a print head alignment or cleaning using the printer’s maintenance tools. Ensure you’re using the correct paper type and settings for the best results.
