## Resolving Common Problems

Despite the robust design of the Canon PIXMA E560 and its driver, users may encounter occasional issues. Awareness of common problems and their solutions can enhance the overall user experience. Some frequent issues include:

1. **Printer Not Recognized:** If the printer is not recognized by your computer, ensure that the driver is correctly installed and that your USB or network connection is securely established.

2. **Low Print Quality:** If printouts are streaky or faded, check for updated drivers, clean the print heads, and verify that the appropriate paper type is selected in the settings.

3. **Connection Problems:** Wireless printing can sometimes fail due to network issues. Ensure that your printer and computer are connected to the same Wi-Fi network and that the printer is within range of the router.

4. **Printer Offline Errors:** If the printer shows an "offline" status, this could be a driver issue. Reinstalling the driver or restarting both the printer and computer may resolve the problem.
