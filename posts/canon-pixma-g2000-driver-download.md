## Troubleshooting Tips

Even with a reliable printer like the Canon PIXMA G2000, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some troubleshooting tips to resolve common problems:

- **Printer Not Recognized:** If the printer is not detected by your computer, ensure that the USB cable is securely connected and that the printer is powered on. Restart both the printer and the computer as a first step.
- **Print Quality Issues:** If prints appear faded or streaked, check the ink levels and perform a print head cleaning via the printer settings on your computer.
- **Installation Errors:** If the driver fails to install correctly, try disabling any antivirus software temporarily during installation and make sure all previous versions of drivers are uninstalled.
- **Paper Jams:** Regularly check for any obstructions in the paper feed mechanism and ensure that the paper is loaded correctly to avoid jams.
