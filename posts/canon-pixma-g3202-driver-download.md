While the Canon PIXMA G3202 is designed to deliver reliable performance, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some troubleshooting tips to help you navigate potential challenges:

- **Paper Jams**: If the printer is experiencing frequent paper jams, check to ensure that the paper is loaded correctly, and that you are using the recommended paper types according to the specifications.
