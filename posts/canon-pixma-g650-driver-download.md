Even with a well-installed printer driver, users may encounter issues that could hinder their printing experience. Here are some troubleshooting tips to address common problems:

1. **Printer Not Recognized**: If the printer does not appear on your computer, ensure that physical connections are secure. For wireless connections, confirm that both the printer and computer are on the same network.

5. **Firewall or Security Software Interference**: Sometimes, security software may block printer connections. Temporarily disable these applications to check if they are causing the issue.
