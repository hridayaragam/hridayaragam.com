## Troubleshooting Tips

Despite its robust performance and user-friendly installation, users may occasionally encounter issues with the Canon PIXMA GM4050. Here are some troubleshooting tips to resolve common problems:

1. **Printer Not Detected**: Ensure that the printer is powered on and connected to the same network as your computer. Restart both the printer and the computer to refresh the connection.

2. **Poor Print Quality**: Check the ink levels and perform a print head cleaning via the printer’s settings menu. Using the recommended paper type can also improve print quality.

3. **Paper Jams**: Remove any jammed paper carefully from the printer. Regularly check and clean the input tray and output slot to prevent future jams.

4. **Driver Issues**: If you experience issues related to the printer driver, consider reinstalling it. Uninstall the existing driver from your computer, then download the latest version again.
