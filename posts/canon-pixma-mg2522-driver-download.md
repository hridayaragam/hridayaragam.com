Despite its robust performance, users may encounter some common issues while using the Canon PIXMA MG2522. Here are a few of them along with their solutions:

1. **Printer Not Recognized**: If your computer does not recognize the printer, check the USB connection and ensure that the printer is powered on. Reinstalling the driver can also solve this issue.

5. **Slow Printing Speed**: If print jobs are taking too long, ensure that you are using the correct print settings and try restarting your printer and computer.
