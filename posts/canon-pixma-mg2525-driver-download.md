## Common Issues and Solutions

While the Canon PIXMA MG2525 generally operates smoothly, users may encounter occasional issues. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

- **Driver Installation Errors**: If you experience difficulties during installation, ensure that your operating system is compatible and that there is sufficient disk space on your computer. Restarting both the printer and computer can also help resolve temporary glitches.

- **Paper Jams**: To resolve paper jams, carefully remove the jammed paper from the printer. Always ensure that the paper tray is loaded correctly and not overfilled.
