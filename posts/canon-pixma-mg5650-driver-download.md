## Troubleshooting Tips

Despite its user-friendly design, users may occasionally encounter issues with the Canon PIXMA MG5650. Below are some common troubleshooting tips to help resolve potential problems:

1. **Connection Issues**: If the printer is not detected by your computer, check the Wi-Fi connection. Ensure the printer and computer are on the same network and try rebooting both devices.

2. **Print Quality Problems**: In the event of streaky or faded prints, perform a print head cleaning using the printer's utility software. Additionally, check ink levels to ensure sufficient supply.

3. **Paper Jams**: If the printer stops working due to a paper jam, carefully remove any stuck paper. Check the input tray and paper path to ensure proper loading and detect any obstacles.

4. **Software Conflicts**: If the printer is not responding, it could be due to software conflicts. Restart your computer, close any unnecessary applications, and try printing again.

5. **Reinstall Drivers**: If issues persist, consider uninstalling and then reinstalling the printer driver. This can resolve corruption or misconfiguration that might have occurred during the initial installation.
