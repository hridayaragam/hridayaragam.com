## Common Issues and Solutions

Despite its advanced features, users of the Canon PIXMA MG6650 may encounter some common issues. Here are a few problems along with their solutions:

- **Driver Installation Failure**: If the driver installation does not complete successfully, ensure that you have administrative rights on your computer. Disable any antivirus software temporarily, as it may interfere with the installation process.
