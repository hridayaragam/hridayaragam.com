## Troubleshooting Tips

Even with a well-functioning printer, users may encounter issues from time to time. Below are some troubleshooting tips for common problems associated with the Canon PIXMA TR4660:

- **Driver Installation Issues**: If the driver fails to install, ensure that you have downloaded the appropriate version compatible with your operating system. Restarting the computer may also resolve the problem.

- **Printer Not Responding**: For cases where the printer is unresponsive, verify that all connections are secure and that the printer is powered on. Additionally, check if it is set as the default printer on your computer.

- **Paper Jams**: Should a paper jam occur, carefully remove any jammed paper, ensuring no remnants are left in the printer. Always use the right type of paper to prevent jams.

- **Wireless Connectivity Issues**: If experiencing difficulties with wireless printing, check the Wi-Fi connection, ensuring the printer and computer are on the same network. Restarting both devices can often restore connectivity.
