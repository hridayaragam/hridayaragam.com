## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the correct printer driver installed, users may occasionally encounter issues while using the Canon PIXMA TS205. Below are common problems and their solutions:

1. **Printer Not Responding**: If the printer does not respond to print commands, check the connections and ensure the printer is powered on. Restart both the printer and the computer to refresh the connection.

5. **Print Jobs in Queue**: If print jobs are stuck in the queue, clear the print spooler and restart the printing process.
