## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the proper setup and installation, users may occasionally encounter issues with their Canon PIXMA TS304. Here are some common problems and suggested solutions:

1. **Printer Not Recognized:** If your computer fails to recognize the printer, ensure that the USB connection is secure or that the Wi-Fi settings are correctly configured. Restarting both the printer and the computer can also resolve the issue.

2. **Print Quality Issues:** For problems related to print quality, such as streaky prints or faded colors, check the ink levels and perform a print head cleaning through the printer’s maintenance settings. Regularly updating the driver can also prevent these issues.

3. **Connectivity Problems:** If the printer frequently disconnects from the network, consider checking your router settings or repositioning the printer for better signal strength. Also, ensure that the printer's firmware is updated, as this can affect network performance.

4. **Slow Printing:** Should you experience unusually slow printing, verify that print jobs are not queued excessively. Clearing the print spooler or adjusting print quality settings can help improve speed.

By promptly addressing these common problems, users can ensure consistent and reliable performance from their Canon PIXMA TS304 printer.
