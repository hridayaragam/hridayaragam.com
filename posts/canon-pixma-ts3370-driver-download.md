## Troubleshooting Tips

Even with a well-installed driver, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some troubleshooting tips to help resolve common problems:

1. **Printer Not Recognized**: If your PC does not recognize the printer, ensure the USB cable is securely connected, or check your Wi-Fi network if using a wireless connection. Restart the printer and the computer to refresh connections.

2. **Print Jobs Stuck in Queue**: Go to the print queue settings on your computer and delete any jammed jobs. Restart the printer and send a new print job to reset the queue.

3. **Poor Print Quality**: Low ink levels or clogged nozzles can result in poor quality. Check the ink levels via the driver interface and perform a print head cleaning cycle if necessary.

4. **Driver Update**: If you experience slow performance or older functionality, checking for a driver update might resolve underlying issues. Regular updates ensure compatibility with your operating system and the latest features.
