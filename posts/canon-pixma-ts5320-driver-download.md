## Troubleshooting Tips

While the Canon PIXMA TS5320 is designed for efficiency, users may encounter occasional issues. Here are some common troubleshooting tips to resolve potential problems:

- **Printer Not Recognized**: Ensure that the printer is correctly connected to the Wi-Fi network. Reboot the printer and router if necessary. If you're using a USB connection, check the cable for damage or try reconnecting it.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If print quality is lacking, consider running a print head cleaning cycle through the printer settings. Regular cleaning can help maintain optimal performance.

- **Paper Jams**: Clear any paper jams by carefully removing the paper from the printer's rear or front access points. Always ensure the paper is loaded correctly and within the printer’s specifications.

- **Driver Issues**: If the printer is not functioning after installation, ensure that the correct driver is installed for your operating system. Reinstalling the driver can often resolve these issues.

- **Check Ink Levels**: Regularly check ink levels through the printer's interface or software. Replace cartridges as needed to avoid print quality issues.
