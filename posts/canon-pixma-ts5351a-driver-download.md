## Troubleshooting Tips

Even with the right driver, users may encounter issues during operation. Here are some troubleshooting tips to resolve common problems:

- **Driver Not Detected**: Ensure that the driver is installed correctly and that the printer is powered on. Restart your device if necessary.

- **Print Jobs Sticking**: If print jobs get stuck in the queue, cancel the jobs from the print menu and try sending the print command again.

- **Poor Print Quality**: Check ink levels and perform a nozzle check. If streaks or missing colors appear, consider running a print head cleaning cycle.

- **Connectivity Issues**: For wireless connections, ensure that the printer and the computer are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Restarting the router may also help resolve intermittent connectivity.

- **Error Messages**: Refer to the printer’s display for specific error codes. The user manual or Canon’s support website can provide guidance on resolving specific error messages.
