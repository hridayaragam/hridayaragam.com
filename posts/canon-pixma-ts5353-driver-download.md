## Common Issues and Solutions

While the installation process for the Canon PIXMA TS5353 driver is generally smooth, users might encounter some common issues. Here are a few potential problems and their solutions:

- **Driver Not Detected:** If your computer does not recognize the printer after installation, ensure that the printer is powered on and properly connected (either via USB or network). Rebooting the computer and printer may resolve the issue.

- **Print Jobs Stuck in Queue:** If print jobs appear to be stuck, clear the print queue and re-send your documents. This issue can also be addressed by restarting the print spooler service on your computer.

- **Poor Print Quality:** If you're experiencing smudged or faded prints, try cleaning the printhead through the printer software. Additionally, make sure you are using the correct paper type and quality settings in the print preferences.

- **Network Connectivity Problems:** For wireless connections, ensure that the printer is connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your computer. Checking router settings and re-establishing the print connection can help resolve this issue.
