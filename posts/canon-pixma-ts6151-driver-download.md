Even with a robust driver installed, users may encounter issues that can hinder the printing experience. Below are some troubleshooting tips to address common problems:

- **Printer Not Recognized**: If the printer is not recognized by the computer, ensure all connections are secure, both for USB and Wi-Fi. Restart the printer and computer to refresh the connection.

- **Slow Printing Speed**: If the printer is operating slower than expected, check if it is set to draft mode in the print settings, as this can affect speed. Adjusting to a higher quality option may improve performance.

- **Driver Compatibility**: In cases where the driver appears outdated, visit the Canon website and download the latest version compatible with your operating system.
