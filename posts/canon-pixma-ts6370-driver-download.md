## Troubleshooting Tips

Despite its advanced features, users may occasionally encounter issues with their Canon PIXMA TS6370 printer. Below are some troubleshooting tips to help resolve common problems:

1. **Connectivity Issues**: If the printer is not connecting to the network, verify the Wi-Fi settings on both the printer and the router. Reconnecting the printer to the network and restarting the router can often resolve these issues.

2. **Poor Print Quality**: If prints appear faded or streaked, it’s advisable to check the ink levels and perform a print head cleaning through the printer’s maintenance settings. 

3. **Driver Errors**: If the printer isn't recognized by the computer, ensure that the correct driver is installed and that it is the latest version compatible with your operating system. Reinstalling the driver might resolve such issues.

4. **Paper Jams**: Paper jams can often happen. Carefully check the paper path and remove any stuck paper without damaging the internal components of the printer.
