## Common Issues and Solutions

While the Canon PIXMA TS7450 is a reliable printer, users may occasionally encounter some common issues. Below are a few troubleshooting tips to help resolve these problems:

- **Printer Not Detected**: Ensure that the printer is powered on, properly connected to the same network as your computer, and that the driver is correctly installed. Restarting both the printer and computer often resolves these connection issues.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If prints appear faded or streaky, check the ink levels and perform a printer head cleaning via the maintenance settings found in the printer software. This action can often resolve issues relating to missed ink channels.

- **Paper Jams**: If paper gets stuck, turn off the printer and carefully open the paper tray to remove any jammed paper gently without tearing it. Restart the printer once cleared.

- **Errors During Installation**: If you encounter error messages during the installation, ensure that any previous drivers are completely uninstalled before initiating the download again. Likewise, temporarily disabling antivirus software during installation can prevent unnecessary interruptions.
