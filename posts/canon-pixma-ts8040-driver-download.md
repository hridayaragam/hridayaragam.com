### What should I do if my printer is not responding after driver installation?

If the printer remains unresponsive post-installation, first check all connections—ensure that cables are securely connected and that the printer is powered on. If using a wireless connection, verify that the printer is connected to the correct Wi-Fi network. Restart both the printer and computer, and attempt to print again. 

### How can I check if I have the latest driver installed?

You can verify the driver version installed on your computer by navigating to the device settings in your operating system. In Windows, go to Devices and Printers, right-click on the Canon PIXMA TS8040, and select 'Printer properties' to see the driver information. For macOS, go to System Preferences, select Printers & Scanners, and check the driver version under the printer list.
