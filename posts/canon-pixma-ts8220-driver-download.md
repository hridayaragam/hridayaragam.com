## Common Issues and Solutions

Despite its many advantages, users may encounter some common issues during printer operation or driver installation. Here are a few potential problems and their solutions:

- **Driver Installation Failure**: If the driver fails to install, ensure that you have downloaded the correct version for your operating system. Additionally, check for system updates and restart your computer before attempting installation again.

- **Printer Not Responding**: If the printer fails to respond, confirm that it is powered on and properly connected either by USB or Wi-Fi. Reconnecting the USB cable or resetting the Wi-Fi connection may resolve the issue.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If printouts are streaked or blurry, consider checking the ink levels. Replace any low or empty cartridges. Running the printer's cleaning utility can also help maintain print quality.

- **Connectivity Issues**: In cases where the printer is not detected over Wi-Fi, ensure that your router is functioning correctly and that the printer is connected to the same network. Restart both the printer and the router as a troubleshooting step.
