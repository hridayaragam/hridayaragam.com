## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the right driver installed, users may encounter some common issues. Understanding how to troubleshoot these problems can help ensure a smoother printing experience. Here are common problems associated with the Canon PIXMA TS9050 and their potential solutions:

1. **Printer Not Detected**: If your computer does not recognize the printer, ensure that it is correctly connected to the network or directly via USB. Restart both the printer and the computer to refresh the connection.

2. **Poor Print Quality**: If you notice streaks or faded prints, it may be due to low ink levels or a clogged print head. Performing a print head cleaning through the printer settings can resolve this issue.

3. **Driver Conflicts**: Sometimes existing drivers may conflict with new installations. Uninstall any previous drivers and reinstall the current version to avoid conflict.

4. **Software Updates**: Keeping your printer and computer software up to date is crucial. Regularly check for updates to both the driver and your operating system.
