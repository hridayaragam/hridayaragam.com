## Troubleshooting Tips

While the Canon PIXMA TS9150 driver is designed for ease of use, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some troubleshooting tips to resolve common problems:

- **Driver Not Recognized**: Ensure that the printer is powered on and properly connected to your network or computer. You may need to restart both the printer and your computer.

- **Print Jobs Stuck in Queue**: Check the print queue for any stalled print jobs. Clear the queue and try printing again.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If colors appear faded or misaligned, consider running a print head cleaning cycle through the printer settings. Additionally, ensure that you are using the correct media type in the print settings.

- **Cannot Connect to Wi-Fi**: Confirm that your Wi-Fi network is functioning properly. If the printer is not connecting, you may need to re-enter the Wi-Fi credentials in the printer's settings.

- **Frequent Driver Updates**: Regularly check for driver updates to enhance performance and fix known issues, ensuring smooth operation over time.
