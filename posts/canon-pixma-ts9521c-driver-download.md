- **Connection Issues**: If you are unable to connect the printer to your computer or network:

- **Print Quality Problems**: Issues like streaks or faded prints can occur over time:

- **Driver Errors**: If you experience error messages related to the printer driver:
  - Verify that the installed driver version is the latest one from the manufacturer.
