Even with an efficient installation and updated driver, users may encounter issues with their Canon PIXMA TS9541C. Below are some troubleshooting tips to resolve common problems:

- **Printer Not Recognized**: If your computer doesn’t recognize the printer, try checking your USB or network connection to ensure it is properly set up. Restarting both the printer and the computer can also help.

- **Print Jobs Stuck**: If print jobs are stuck in the queue, clearing the queue and restarting the print spooler service can often resolve the issue. This can typically be done through the Control Panel on Windows systems.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If you notice lines or fading in your prints, it may be time to clean the print head. Most Canon printers come with a utility in the driver software that provides options for maintenance tasks.

- **Driver Conflicts**: If you experience errors or unexpected behavior, it may be beneficial to uninstall the current driver and perform a clean installation of the latest version.

- **Check for Updates**: Regularly check the Canon website or the provided link for any available updates to ensure your printer continues to perform optimally.
