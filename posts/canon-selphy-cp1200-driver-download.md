## Common Issues and Solutions

Despite its many benefits, users may encounter certain challenges when using the Canon SELPHY CP1200. Here are common issues and solutions:

- **Driver Installation Failures**: If the driver fails to install, ensure you have downloaded the right version for your operating system. Temporarily disable any antivirus software that may interfere during the installation.

- **Connection Problems**: For users attempting to connect via Wi-Fi, ensure you are within range of the router. Consider resetting the printer's Wi-Fi settings and reconnecting to the network.

- **Slow Printing Speed**: If the printing process is slower than usual, check if any large files are in the queue and clear them. Additionally, ensure that your device meets the printer’s requirements.

- **Compatibility Issues**: If your device does not recognize the printer, ensure the driver is installed correctly and that the printer is powered on. Restarting the computer and printer can often resolve recognition issues.
