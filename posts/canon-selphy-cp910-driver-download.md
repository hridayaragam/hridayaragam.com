### 1. Printer Not Recognized:
- Ensure that the printer is turned on and properly connected to your device. If using Wi-Fi, verify that both devices are on the same network.

- Many error messages can be resolved by consulting the user manual or Canon’s support website for troubleshooting tips.
- Restarting both the printer and the connected device can often resolve transient errors.
