## Troubleshooting Tips

Even with reliable technology, issues may occasionally arise. Here are some troubleshooting tips to resolve common problems with the Dell 1130n printer:

- **Driver Issues**: If the printer fails to communicate with your computer, ensure that the correct driver is installed. Re-download and reinstall the driver if needed.
