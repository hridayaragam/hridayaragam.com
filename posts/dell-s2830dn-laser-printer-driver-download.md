1. **Printer Not Detected:** If your computer does not recognize the printer, check the connection. Ensure that all cables are secured and that the printer is powered on. Restarting both your computer and the printer can also help reset any connections.

2. **Poor Print Quality:** If you notice faded or streaked prints, check the ink or toner levels. Ensure that you have the correct driver installed that supports high-quality printing for the S2830DN model.

3. **Queue Issues:** If print jobs are stuck in the queue, try clearing the print queue. You can do this by navigating to "Devices and Printers" on your computer, right-clicking the S2830DN printer, and selecting "See what's printing." From there, you can cancel the stuck print jobs.

4. **Driver Conflicts:** Sometimes, new updates or installations can result in driver conflicts. If you suspect this, uninstall the current driver and reinstall a fresh copy from the driver download page.

5. **Error Messages:** Consult the printer’s display panel for any specific error messages. These messages can often provide insight into what is wrong, whether it’s out of paper, a paper jam, or a maintenance notice.
