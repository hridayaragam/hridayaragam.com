## Common Issues and Solutions

Despite its robust design, users may occasionally encounter issues when using the HP Color LaserJet Enterprise M750n and its driver. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

- **Printer Not Detected**: If your computer fails to recognize the printer, ensure both devices are connected to the same network. You may also try reinstalling the driver or connecting via USB as a temporary solution.

- **Slow Printing**: If printing speeds are slower than expected, check for any pending print jobs in the queue and clear any stuck jobs. Restarting the printer can also help refresh its performance.

- **Driver Errors**: If the driver encounters errors, try uninstalling and then reinstalling the latest driver version. This can help eliminate any corrupted files that may be affecting performance.
