## Troubleshooting Tips

Even with the right driver installed, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some common problems along with their troubleshooting solutions:

- **Printer Not Recognized**: If your computer does not recognize the printer, ensure that the USB cable is securely connected. Try using a different USB port on your computer or restart both the printer and the computer.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If prints appear streaky or faded, check the ink levels. You may also need to clean the print head through the printer settings.

- **Connection Issues**: Unstable communication between the printer and computer can arise from outdated drivers. Always ensure your driver is up to date. If needed, uninstall the existing driver and reinstall the latest one.

- **Error Messages**: If you encounter error codes, refer to the printer’s manual or the HP support website. Often, these messages provide guidance on how to rectify specific issues.
