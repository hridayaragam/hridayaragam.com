## Resolving Common Problems

Even with a functional driver, users may occasionally encounter issues. Below are some common problems and their potential solutions:

- **Printer Not Found**: If your computer cannot detect the printer, ensure that the printer is powered on and connected to the same network as your device. Restarting both the printer and the computer can also help.

- **Printing Errors**: If print jobs are getting stuck or failing, checking the print queue for any pending jobs can resolve the issue. Clearing the print queue and restarting the printer may restore normal operation.
