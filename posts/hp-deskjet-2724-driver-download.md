## Common Issues and Solutions

While the HP DeskJet 2724 is designed for reliability, users may encounter common issues that are easy to resolve:

### Printer Not Responding

If the printer does not respond when sent a print command, ensure that the driver is correctly installed. Reinstall the driver if necessary, and check the connection between the printer and the computer.

### Poor Print Quality

Blurry or faded prints may result from low ink levels or a clogged printer head. Check the ink cartridges for sufficient levels, and if needed, run the printer's cleaning utility.

### Connectivity Issues

For wireless connections, ensure that both the printer and the computer are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Restarting both the printer and the router can often resolve connectivity problems.

### Error Messages

Error messages can often be traced back to outdated drivers or miscommunications between devices. Make sure that the driver is up-to-date and retry printing jobs after power cycling the devices.
