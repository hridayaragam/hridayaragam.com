## Common Issues and Solutions

Even with its many benefits, users may encounter some common issues when using the HP Deskjet 3520. Here are a few problems along with their corresponding solutions:

1. **Printer Not Detected**: If your computer fails to recognize the printer, ensure that all cables are correctly plugged in or that the printer is connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your device. Restart both devices and try again.
   
2. **Poor Print Quality**: If you notice faded prints or streaks, check the ink levels and consider cleaning the print heads through the printer settings. You may also need to use high-quality paper for optimal results.

3. **Driver Issues**: Sometimes the driver installation may not complete successfully. In such cases, uninstall the current driver via the Control Panel and re-download the latest version from the official site.

4. **Paper Jams**: Frequent paper jams can disrupt printing. Ensure that the paper is properly loaded into the tray and that the paper type is compatible with the printer settings.

5. **Slow Printing Speed**: If the printer is running slowly, check for network congestion. For USB connections, ensure that the connection is secure and that other applications are not using excessive system resources.

Addressing these issues promptly can help maintain the performance of the HP Deskjet 3520, ensuring that it serves your needs effectively.
