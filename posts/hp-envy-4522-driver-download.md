## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the best driver, users may face issues ranging from connectivity problems to print quality concerns. Here are some common problems and their potential solutions:

- **Connection Issues**: If your printer is not recognized by your computer, check that it is properly connected via USB or that it’s connected to the correct Wi-Fi network. Restart both devices if necessary.

- **Print Quality Issues**: If your prints are streaky or faded, consider running a printer maintenance utility, which is often included in the driver software. Cleaning the print heads can solve such problems.

- **Driver Not Found**: If your computer claims it cannot find the driver, ensure that you have downloaded the correct version for your operating system. Reinstalling the driver may also resolve this issue.

- **Print Queue Jamming**: Sometimes, jobs get stuck in the print queue. Cancel the stuck jobs and restart the printer and computer to reset the queue.
