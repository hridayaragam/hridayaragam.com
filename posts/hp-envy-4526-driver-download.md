## Resolving Common Problems

Despite the efficiency of the HP ENVY 4526, users may occasionally encounter hurdles even after a successful driver installation. Here are some common issues and their potential resolutions:

1. **Printer Not Recognized by Computer**: Ensure that the printer is turned on and properly connected to your computer via USB or network connection. Check to see if the driver installation completed successfully and if the correct driver version is being used.

2. **Print Jobs Stuck in Queue**: Sometimes, print jobs can become stalled. Access the printer settings on your computer, navigate to the print queue, and clear any stuck jobs. Restarting both the printer and computer can also help.

3. **Poor Print Quality**: If you notice faded or streaked prints, check your ink levels and consider running a printer maintenance routine or cleaning cycles, which can often be accessed through the driver settings.

4. **Connection Issues**: If using a wireless connection, ensure that both the printer and the computer are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Restarting the network router and re-establishing the connection may resolve any disruptions.
