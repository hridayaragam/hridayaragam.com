## Common Issues and Solutions

Despite its reliability, users may occasionally encounter issues with the HP ENVY 5548. Below are some common problems along with their respective solutions:

### Problem: Printer Not Detected
**Solution**: Ensure that the printer is powered on and connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your computer. Restart both devices and check the network settings.

### Problem: Poor Print Quality
**Solution**: Check the ink levels to see if the cartridges need replacing. Running a print head cleaning cycle from the printer’s settings may also help.

### Problem: Installation Failure
**Solution**: Ensure that your operating system is compatible and that you have administrative rights for the installation. Temporarily disable any antivirus software during installation.

### Problem: Paper Jams
**Solution**: Remove any jammed paper carefully and ensure that the paper tray is loaded correctly. Regularly clean the paper feed rollers to prevent future jams.
