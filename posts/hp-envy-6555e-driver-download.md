## Troubleshooting Tips

Even with proper installation, users may encounter issues when using the HP Envy 6555e. Below are some troubleshooting tips to resolve common problems:

- **Driver Not Recognized**: If the printer driver is not recognized, ensure that the printer is properly connected to your computer or network. Restart both the printer and computer to reset connections.
- **Print Jobs Stuck in Queue**: Occasionally, print jobs may become stuck in the queue. In such a case, right-click the printer icon in the control panel and select ‘See what’s printing’. Clear any stuck jobs and try printing again.
- **Poor Print Quality**: If the printed output is unsatisfactory, check the ink levels and ensure that you are using the correct paper type. Running a print head cleaning through the printer utility can also help.
- **Connectivity Issues**: For printers connected via Wi-Fi, verify that both your printer and computer are on the same network. Restart the router if necessary and ensure that the printer is connected to Wi-Fi.

By following these troubleshooting tips, users can effectively resolve many common printing issues, maintaining a seamless printing experience.
