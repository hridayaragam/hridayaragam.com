## Common Issues and Solutions

Despite its functionality, users may encounter issues with the HP ENVY Pro 6430 from time to time. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

- **Driver Installation Failure**: Sometimes, users may experience difficulties during driver installation. Ensure that you are downloading the correct driver for your operating system. Restart your computer and try the installation again.

- **Printer Not Recognized**: If the printer is not recognized by your computer, check the connectivity. For USB connections, ensure the cable is securely connected. For wireless setups, confirm that the printer and computer are on the same network.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If you notice streaks or faded prints, it may be due to low ink levels or clogged printheads. Check ink levels and perform a printhead cleaning via the printer's settings.

- **Slow Printing Speeds**: If the printer is printing slowly, check for any background tasks that might be consuming system resources. Ensure that the printer is connected to a strong Wi-Fi signal or try using a wired connection.
