## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the optimal driver installed, users may encounter challenges. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

1. **Printer Not Detected**: If your computer does not recognize the printer, verify that the connection (USB or Wi-Fi) is secure. Ensure your printer is powered on and in the correct mode for connectivity.

2. **Print Jobs Stuck in Queue**: If print jobs are not processed, clear the print queue by going to ‘Devices and Printers’ in the control panel, right-clicking the printer icon, and selecting ‘See what’s printing’. From there, you can cancel or restart jobs.

3. **Poor Print Quality**: If you notice smudged or faded prints, make sure that the ink levels are adequate. Run a printer cleaning cycle from the maintenance section of the printer software.

4. **Driver Compatibility Issues**: If you have recently upgraded your operating system, an outdated driver may not function correctly. Check for updates regularly and download the latest version from the HP support page.
