While installing and using the HP Laser MFP 135w driver can be straightforward, users may occasionally encounter issues. Below are some common problems and solutions:

### Problem: Printer Not Detected

**Solution**: Ensure that the printer is powered on and connected to the same network as your computer if you’re using a wireless connection. If using USB, check that the cable is securely connected.

### Problem: Slow Printing Speed

**Solution**: Check if the printer is set to draft mode instead of standard or high-quality mode. Additionally, ensure that your device's RAM and processing power are adequate for the tasks.

### Problem: Print Jobs Stuck in Queue

**Solution**: Restart the print spooler service on your computer. This can often clear stuck jobs. If the problem persists, reinstalling the driver may be necessary.

### Problem: Outdated Driver

**Solution**: Regularly check the HP website for driver updates. Upgrading to the latest version can resolve issues and enhance performance.
