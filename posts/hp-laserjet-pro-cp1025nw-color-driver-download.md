## Resolving Common Problems

Despite the reliability of the HP LaserJet Pro CP1025nw, users may encounter various issues from time to time. Below are common problems and how to resolve them effectively:

- **Printer Not Recognized**: Make sure the driver is properly installed and that you are using the correct USB port or network connection. Check if the printer appears in your computer’s device list.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If documents are printing with streaks or unclear text, consider updating the driver. Additionally, inspect the toner cartridges and ensure they are not empty or clogged.

- **Connectivity Issues**: For users connected via Wi-Fi, ensure that the printer is on the same network as your computer. Check router settings or printer settings to ensure a stable connection.

- **Frequent Paper Jams**: Ensure that paper is loaded correctly and that the type of paper being used is suitable for the printer. Regular maintenance, such as cleaning the feed rollers, can also mitigate this issue.
