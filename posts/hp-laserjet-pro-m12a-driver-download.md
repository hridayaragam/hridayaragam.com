## Common Issues and Solutions

While the HP LaserJet Pro M12a is designed for efficiency, users may encounter some common issues during operation. Here are potential problems and their corresponding solutions:

- **Driver Installation Fails**: If the driver fails to install, ensure that the correct driver version is downloaded. Also, check for any administrative restrictions on your device that may hinder installation.

- **Printer Not Detected**: If the printer is not recognized by your computer, ensure the USB connection is secure, or verify that your wireless settings are correctly configured. Restarting both the printer and computer can also resolve connectivity issues.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If print quality declines, consider checking the toner cartridge levels and cleaning the printer's internal components as per the user manual instructions. Regular maintenance can prevent performance drops.
