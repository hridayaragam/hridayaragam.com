## Troubleshooting Tips

While the HP LaserJet Pro M403dn is a reliable printer, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some common troubleshooting tips:

1. **Printer Not Recognized**: If your computer fails to recognize the printer, check the connections. Ensure the USB cable or network connection is secure. Restart the printer and computer if necessary.
2. **Poor Print Quality**: For subpar print quality, inspect the toner cartridges for low levels. If necessary, replace the cartridge. Additionally, check the printer’s maintenance settings to run a cleaning cycle.
3. **Paper Jams**: To resolve paper jams, carefully remove any stuck paper from the paper path. Check for any torn pieces that may cause future jams.
4. **Slow Printing**: If the printer is slower than expected, check for large print jobs queued in the printer settings. Cancel any unnecessary jobs that might be causing delays.
5. **Driver Issues**: If printing fails, ensure that you have installed the correct driver for your operating system. Reinstall the driver if necessary.

By applying these troubleshooting tips, users can often resolve common issues and maintain optimal printer performance.
