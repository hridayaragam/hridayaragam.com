## FAQ and Support

### What should I do if my printer isn’t recognized after driver installation?
If your printer is not recognized after installing the driver, try unplugging it and reconnecting it. Also, check the connection type (USB or wireless) and ensure that the correct port is selected in your computer's printer settings. 

### How do I revert to an older driver version?
If you encounter issues with the latest driver, you can revert to a previous version by navigating to the Device Manager in Windows, finding your printer, and selecting the option to roll back the driver.

### Where can I find troubleshooting help?

### Is it necessary to uninstall the previous driver before installing a new one?
