## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the right driver, users may occasionally encounter issues with the HP LaserJet Ultra M206dn. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

1. **Printed Pages are Blurry**: This can occur due to incorrect print settings. Ensure that you select the appropriate print quality settings in the print dialog. Additionally, check that the toner cartridge is correctly installed and has not run low on toner.

2. **Printer Not Recognized**: If the printer isn’t recognized by your computer, ensure that the USB connection is secure or that the Wi-Fi network settings are correctly configured. Restarting both your printer and computer can also help.

3. **Frequent Paper Jams**: Regular paper jams may indicate an issue with the paper type or loading. Ensure that the correct paper is being used and that the tray is loaded properly, without exceeding the maximum capacity.

4. **Slow Printing Speed**: If print jobs are taking longer than expected, check if the printer is in Power Saver mode, as this can slow down performance. Adjust settings in the driver software to optimize print speed for your needs.
