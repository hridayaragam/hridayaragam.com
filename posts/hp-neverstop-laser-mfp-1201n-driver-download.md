## Troubleshooting Tips

Even with the reliability of the HP Neverstop Laser MFP 1201n, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some troubleshooting tips to help resolve common problems:

1. **Printer Not Recognized**: Ensure that the printer is connected to the correct network or via USB. Restart both the printer and the computer to refresh the connection.

2. **Poor Print Quality**: Check whether the toner is properly loaded and that there are no obstructions in the toner path. Running a cleaning cycle from the printer's settings may also improve print quality.

3. **Slow Printing**: If the printer is taking an unusually long time to print, check for large print jobs in the queue. Clear any excess jobs, and consider reducing print quality for faster output.

4. **Paper Jams**: If a paper jam occurs, carefully remove the jammed paper, ensuring no bits are left inside the printer. Refer to the user manual for specific instructions on addressing jams.
