## Common Issues and Solutions

While the HP Officejet 4610 is generally reliable, users may occasionally encounter issues. Below are some common problems and their solutions:

- **Connection Problems**: If the printer is not connecting to your computer, ensure that the USB cable is securely plugged in or that your wireless network is functioning correctly. Restarting the printer and router may also resolve the issue.

- **Slow Printing Speed**: Slow performance may be attributed to large print jobs or high-resolution settings. Reduce the print quality to draft mode for quicker prints when the best quality is not necessary.
