## Troubleshooting Tips

Despite the efficiency and reliability of the HP OfficeJet 5264, users may occasionally encounter issues. Here are some troubleshooting tips to help resolve common problems:

1. **Driver Update**: If you experience printing problems, check to see if the printer driver is up to date. Visit the HP website to download the latest version if needed.
2. **Connection Issues**: For wireless connections, ensure that both the printer and computer are on the same network. Restart your router if needed. For USB connections, inspect the cable for damage and ensure it is securely plugged in.
3. **Printer Queue Problems**: Clear the printer queue if documents are stuck by navigating to your computer's "Devices and Printers" section, right-clicking the printer, and selecting "See what's printing."
4. **Paper Jams**: Inspect the printer for any paper jams, ensuring that the paper is loaded correctly and that there are no obstructions.
5. **Quality Issues**: If the print quality is poor, perform a print head cleaning via the printer settings menu, and ensure you are using the appropriate paper type for the best results.
