## Common Issues and Solutions

While issues may arise during or after the installation of the HP OfficeJet 6958 driver, many can be resolved easily. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

- **Driver Not Found**: If your computer cannot locate the printer driver, ensure that you have downloaded the correct version for your operating system. Restart both the computer and the printer, and attempt the installation again.
