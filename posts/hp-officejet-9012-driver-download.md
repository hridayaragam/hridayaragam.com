## Common Issues and Solutions

While using the HP OfficeJet 9012 driver, users may encounter several common issues. Below are some potential problems along with their solutions:

- **Driver Installation Errors**: If you receive an error during installation, ensure that you have the correct driver for your operating system. Restart your computer and try reinstalling the driver.
- **Printer Not Recognized**: If your computer does not recognize the printer, check the USB connection or ensure that both devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi network for wireless printing. You may also need to restart both the printer and the computer.

- **Paper Jam or Print Quality Issues**: Regular maintenance is crucial for preventing paper jams. Ensure that the printer is loaded with the correct type of paper, and clean the print heads if quality issues arise.
