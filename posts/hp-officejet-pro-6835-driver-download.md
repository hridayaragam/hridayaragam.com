## Troubleshooting Tips

While the HP Officejet Pro 6835 is designed for reliable operation, users may encounter occasional issues. Below are some helpful troubleshooting tips to resolve common problems:

1. **Printer Not Found**: If your computer cannot detect the printer, ensure that it is powered on and connected to the same network as your computer. Try restarting both the printer and your computer.

2. **Print Jobs Stuck**: If print jobs get stuck in the queue, cancel them and reattempt printing. Check for paper jams or low ink that could hinder printing.

3. **Poor Print Quality**: If the print quality is unsatisfactory, consider running the printer's built-in maintenance options or checking the ink levels to ensure cartridges are not running low.

4. **Driver Issues**: If you experience persistent problems, consider uninstalling and reinstalling the driver. Ensure that you are using the latest version available.
