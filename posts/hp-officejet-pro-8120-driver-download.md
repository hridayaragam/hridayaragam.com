## Resolving Common Problems

Even with a reliable driver, users may occasionally run into issues with the HP OfficeJet Pro 8120. Here are some common problems and their troubleshooting steps:

1. **Printer Not Recognized**: If your computer fails to recognize the printer, ensure that the correct driver is installed for your operating system, and check the connection (USB or network).

2. **Poor Print Quality**: Problems like streaks or faded prints can often be resolved by running a print head cleaning which can be accessed from the printer settings or software on your computer.

3. **Slow Printing Speeds**: If your printer is taking longer to print than expected, ensure that the printer is not set to draft quality in the settings, as this can significantly alter performance.

4. **Driver Update Errors**: If you encounter issues while updating the driver, uninstalling the existing driver completely and then performing a fresh installation can often resolve the problem.

5. **Paper Jams**: Regular maintenance, including checking for debris and ensuring correct paper loading, can prevent paper jams. If a jam occurs, clear it as per the user manual instructions.
