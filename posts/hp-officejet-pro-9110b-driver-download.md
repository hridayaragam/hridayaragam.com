## Resolving Common Problems

Even with the best driver, users may occasionally encounter issues while using the HP OfficeJet Pro 9110b. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

- **Printer Not Detected**: If your computer doesn’t detect your printer, ensure that both are connected to the same network if you are using Wi-Fi. Restart both devices, and recheck the connection settings.

- **Print Jobs Stuck in Queue**: Sometimes, print jobs may become stuck in the queue. To resolve this, go to the Devices and Printers settings on your computer, select the HP OfficeJet Pro 9110b, and cancel any pending jobs. Restart the printer and try printing again.

- **Poor Print Quality**: If you observe poor print quality, check your ink levels and perform a print head cleaning from the printer settings. Frequent use of the printer can also harm print quality if heads are not cleaned regularly.

- **Error Messages**: Pay attention to any error codes displayed on your printer. Refer to HP’s website or user manual for specific solutions related to error codes, as they can vary.
