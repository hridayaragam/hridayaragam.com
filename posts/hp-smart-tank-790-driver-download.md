## Troubleshooting Tips

Despite the straightforward installation and versatile performance, users may occasionally encounter issues while using their HP Smart Tank 790. Here are some troubleshooting tips to address common problems:

- **Driver Conflicts**: If the printer is not responding, check for driver conflicts or outdated drivers. Reinstalling or updating the driver may resolve these issues.
